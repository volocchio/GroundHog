Helicopter Performance Data Sources

Performance numbers are ISA approximations for flight planning only. Always consult the actual AFM for your serial-number aircraft.

  • S-300C — Schweizer S-300C Rotorcraft Flight Manual (RFM) / Hughes 269C Pilot's Operating Handbook (POH)
  • S300CBi — Schweizer S-300CBi RFM
  • R22 — Robinson R22 Beta II POH (R22 POH Rev. current)
  • R44 — Robinson R44 Raven II POH
  • R66 — Robinson R66 Turbine POH
  • B206 — Bell 206B3 JetRanger III Rotorcraft Flight Manual (BHT-206B3-FM-1)
  • B407 — Bell 407 Rotorcraft Flight Manual (BHT-407-FM-1)
  • H125 — Airbus H125 / AS350 B3e Approved Flight Manual
  • MD500E — MD 500E (Hughes 369E) Rotorcraft Flight Manual
  • F28F — Enstrom 280FX Shark Pilot's Operating Handbook
  • UH-1H — Bell UH-1H Operator's Manual (TM 55-1520-210-10)

HOGE/HIGE max gross weights, rate of climb, cruise speed, and fuel burn are interpolated from published hover ceiling and performance charts at standard density-altitude breakpoints. FAA Type Certificate Data Sheets (TCDS) used for structural limits.
